our story

a sanctuary in the heart of the city

We offer a serene escape from the dissonant city. Based in Vietnamese cuisine, we serve unique flavours inspired by traditional and foreign ingredients we come across. Inhale the aromas of fragrant food and earthly incense. Exhale the weight of the world upon you, your bliss awaits.
