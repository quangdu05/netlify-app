quietly ashamed, I chewed yet struggled to swallow. I thought to myself, if you felt compelled to act from just a simple conversation, how can you combat grander desires? The Buddha taught me that to achieve enlightenment is to be free of mortal desires, and if those who have less can withstand more than me, what does that say about me? Forever lingering in my mind, this question taunted every decision I made.

This is the inspiration behind Shamballa, a haven made from my heart for those seeking to savour the extensive world of flavours within our vegetables. The word "Shamballa" can be traced back to Sanskrit where it means a place of peace, stasis, and joy.

I warmly invite you into my vision of this realm, a sanctuary to detach from your reality and let Tibetan tunes wash over your mind. I pass on their good karma to you, my dearest sentient siblings.
